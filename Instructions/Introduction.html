About the researcher

Welcome to my experiment! I am a master's student in Business Economics - Neuroeconomics at the University of Amsterdam and this is an experiment conducted as part of my thesis project.

About the experiment

The experiment consists of a two-stage investment decision and a brief questionnaire. If you choose to participate, your decisions can lead to payments in addition to your participation fee.

About the selection and processing of your data

Your decisions and information provided will be recorded anonymously, meaning that your response in this experiment cannot be traced back to you.
Your data will be used for academic research only.
You can choose at any point during the experiment to stop your participation and your data will be discarded.

Duration

There is a timer at each stage to ensure that the lasts 10 minutes .
