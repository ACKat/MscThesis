About the researcher

Welcome to my experiment! I am a master's student in Business Economics - Neuroeconomics at the University of Amsterdam and this is an experiment conducted as part of my thesis project.

About the experiment

The experiment consists of a two-stage investment decision and a brief questionnaire. If you choose to participate, you can earn a bonus payment depending on your decisions.

About the selection and processing of your data

Your decisions and information provided will be recorded anonymously, meaning that your response in this experiment cannot be traced back to you.
Your data will be used for academic research exclusively.
You can choose to withdraw your participation at any point during the experiment and your data will be discarded.

Duration

The expected duration of the experiment is 5 to 10 minutes.
Exceeding this time frame or completing the study without paying attention may lead to a return of your submission. If you decide to participate, it is important that you remain focused throught the process.
